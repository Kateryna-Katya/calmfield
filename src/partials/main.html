Interaction through rhythm

Game Idea

Calmfield: Breathing Paths transforms simple puzzle mechanics into a mindful activity. Players don’t rush — they move in sync with a slow rhythm that mirrors natural breathing. It's pretty cool how staying focused and calm actually makes you better at the game - like, the more zen you are, the better you play.

Core Interaction

  • Actions are triggered during inhale and exhale phases
  • Paths expand and contract based on timing
  • Movement must align with rhythm to succeed
  • Mistimed actions gently reset progress

We're trying to get people to slow down and really pay attention, instead of just rushing through everything.

How It Works
